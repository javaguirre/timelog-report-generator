Timelog Report Generator

This is a report generator for text based timelogs, We made it for gtimelog but it could be configured to check for other patterns in the text files to fit your needs.

We should be able to generate weekly, monthly or daily reports using your timelog.txt file and generate it from vimwiki, which is the one I am using for the timetrack.

Example:

python2 timelog.py --start-date 1/1/2013 --end-date 30/6/2013 --order-by week --tasks

Output:

====== Week 17 ======= Project1 : 9 h 30 min * [X] Task completed Total time: 9 h 30 min ====== Week 18 ======= Project3 : 0 h 30 min Project2 : 3 h 36 min * [.] Task started Total time: 4 h 6 min
